### Markdown Tag
The format for displaying images:
``` markdown
![](<path to image> <image title>)
![](/assets/images/2021-08-14/kittycat1.jpg "Image of Cat")
```
The resulting image should look like so:

![](/assets/images/2021-08-14/kittycat1.jpg "Image of Cat")
